About

Our website offers a simple way to extract information from image files, especially invoices. Through our user-friendly interface, users can easily upload image files containing data, such as invoices. Once an image is uploaded, our sophisticated logic automatically processes it to extract the necessary information. It is simple to access and save the extracted data for later use. We also provide a scheduling feature that enables users to designate specific times for data extraction. Because of this, users can plan the extraction process for their preferred time, ensuring maximum convenience and efficiency. With the help of our website, data extraction from image files is simple, effective, and adaptable to each user's needs.
